# Competitor Profiles — 7 Platforms × 14 Dimensions

> Knowledge as of Jan 2026 cutoff. Items I cannot verify are marked **(unverified)**. The goal is to extract *transferable mechanics*, not to catalog every feature.

Dimensions analyzed per platform: **1** Features · **2** UX strengths · **3** UX weaknesses · **4** Retention systems · **5** Habit loops · **6** Community · **7** Wearable integrations · **8** AI · **9** Monetization · **10** Notifications · **11** Gamification · **12** Analytics depth · **13** Personalization · **14** Performance.

## Cross-cutting patterns (what every winner has)

1. **A daily or near-daily loop** with a single hero metric (steps, calories logged, streak, kudos).
2. **A morning "check" ritual** — sleep score, readiness, rings, streak status.
3. **Social accountability** — someone else can see whether you showed up.
4. **AI that reduces friction** (photo logging) or adds insight (readiness, coaching).
5. **Notifications that deliver value or social signal**, not nagging (kudos > "don't forget to log").
6. **Performance as a feature** — the leaders feel instant and native.
7. **A recap/celebration cadence** (weekly digest, Wrapped) that reflects effort back to the user.

We currently have **#1 partially (streak ring)** and **#7 not at all**. The rest are open.
