**Competitors analyzed:** Samsung Health · HealthifyMe · Cult.fit · MyFitnessPal · Strava · Fitbit · Nike Training Club.

**One-line thesis:** We don't out-feature the consumer giants — we win by being the best *gym-attached* app, layering Strava's community psychology and Cult's engagement onto the one thing no competitor has: a verified, real relationship between a member, their gym, and their trainer. The flywheel center is the **verified check-in**.

> Competitor data is from product knowledge as of the Jan 2026 cutoff (items I couldn't verify are labeled **(unverified)**). Our-platform capabilities are verified against the codebase and project memory.
